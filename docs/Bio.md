# This is ClubHub. 

Our mission is to improve the accesibility of information for clubs at CCA. 

With our app, CCA students will be able to smoothly search for clubs and events they are interested in while being provided all the information they need aboutt the clubs.

In our current prototype (as of 4/17/2026), students can navigate the interface and click on clubs that they might be interested in.
In our final design, we are hoping to add a system that suggests clubs or events to students based on their interests and perhaps their recent activity on the app.

With the incredible team of programmers we have at ClubHub, CCA students can expect a simple but detailed app allowing them to find what they truly want to do.
